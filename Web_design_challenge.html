Latitude vs. X

The goal of this project was to demonstrate changes in specific weather phenomena depending on the lattitude of where the data was collected. We used data from OpenWeatherMap API to complete the analysis involving more than 500 randomly chosen cities.

After creating the dataset, we used Matplotlib to plot the result of different weather patterns in regards to latitude. The factors we considered included: cloudines, humidity, maximum temperature, and wind speed. Here you can explore our project, looking at the source data and various visualizations of the analysis along with explanations of the trends and correlations.
